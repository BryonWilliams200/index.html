Exploring DexKit

Discover how DexKit helps students learn about robotic hands, haptic technology, and engineering through interactive activities.

What is DexKit?

DexKit is a platform that allows students to learn about robotic hands, dexterous manipulation, and haptic technology through interactive experiences.

DexKit Interface

The DexKit interface allows users to explore robotic hand designs, control movements, and understand how engineers develop robotic systems.

Haptic Devices

Haptic devices provide touch-based feedback that allows users to experience interaction between humans and robotic systems.

PrusaSlicer

The first step in building the robotic hand is printing the necessary components. PrusaSlicer is free, open-source 3D printing software that prepares models for printing. It allows users to arrange parts on the print bed, optimize the layout, and generate the instructions that a 3D printer follows to create each component.
