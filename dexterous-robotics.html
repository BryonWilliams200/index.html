What is Dexterous Robotics?

Dexterous robotics is the field of robotics focused on creating machines that can perform precise movements and manipulate objects using advanced control systems.

Unlike simple robots that perform repetitive tasks, dexterous robots use sensors, motors, and feedback systems to adjust their movements and interact with objects in more human-like ways.

Key Concepts

  • Tactile sensing
  • Soft robotics
  • Actuation systems
  • Feedback control
  • Object manipulation
